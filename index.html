Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard presents four analytical views of childhood education outcomes and public spending efficiency:

  1. Panel 1 (top-left): Learning poverty ranking by region
  2. Panel 2 (top-right): Relationship between out-of-school rates and completion
  3. Panel 3 (bottom-left): Spending efficiency analysis with frontier visualization
  4. Panel 4 (bottom-right): Equity analysis showing gender gaps by income level

All panels support linked highlighting - selecting a region in one panel highlights it across all views.
